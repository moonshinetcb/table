Overwatch 2 Tank Breakdowns

In the multiplayer team-based shooter Overwatch 2 there are three roles, Tank, Damage, and Support. This table will be briefly analyzing the current characters in the Tank role and analyzing their strengths and weaknesses.

DISCLAMER

I am not a super smart or particularly stellar Overwatch player by any means so do not take these opinions of mine too matter-a-factly. These are just my opinions as a long-time player of Overwatch
