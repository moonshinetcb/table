Overwatch 2 Tank Breakdowns

In the multiplayer team-based shooter Overwatch 2 there are three roles, Tank, Damage, and Support. This table will be analyzing the current characters in the Tank role and analyzing their strengths and weaknesses.

DISCLAMER

I am not a super smart or particularly stellar Overwatch player by any means so do not take these opinions of mine too matter-a-factly. These are just my opinions as a long-time player of Overwatch

Overwatch 2 Tanks
Character Pros Cons
D.Va

High Health, good defensive ability and mobility, if her high health goes to 0 she will not die, she will just get out of her mech Easily countered by characters that can do high damage against her massive mech hitbox, if constantly de-meched, D.va will be practically useless as a character since her damage is heavily nerfed when not in her mech
Doomfist

High mobility, low skill cooldowns, hard to kill or defend against, powerful defensive block that can also power up his Punch ability Punch ability can do low damage if not charged up, hard to use effectively on certain teams
Junker Queen

Strong close-range damage, powerful Ultimate that negates healing, able to heal herself and cause bleeding to enemy players Low health compared to other tanks, harder to use compared to other Tanks, can be killed from a range by several heroes
Orisa

An immovable tank with reliable survivability, a long-range gun and spear, and good damage Easy to hit, slow movement
Reinhardt

Powerful close-range hammer, massive shield that can shield your entire team, powerful ultimate that can stun entire enemy team Massive hitbox, easy to kill from long ranges
Roadhog

Powerful close-range damage, Chain Hook ability that can bring enemies closer to you for an instant-kill, easy self-heal Easy to hit, if the Roadhog misses their Chain Hook ability then they are easy to kill from any range
Sigma

Strong flexible shield, high mid-range damage with primary fire and Accretion ability, powerful Ultimate that takes half of the enemy teams health No mobility at all, easily countered if shield and defensive abilities are down
Winston

High mobility, large bubble shield, Tesla Cannon can kill any Damage character very easily Long range heroes can take Winston out with relative ease, supports and certain damage heroes can counter Winston's damage and kill him easily
Wrecking Ball

Arguably the highest mobility character in the entire game, can get in and out of conflicts with minimal consequences, can move and disorient the enemy team with high mobility and strong close range attacks Hard to use, round hitbox which is easy to hit, and is counterable by stuns and heroes that can track him even from a long range and up close
Zarya

All around good survivability with her Bubble ability that also gives her a damage increase when enemy players hit her, powerful ultimate that can trap most of the characters in the game No mobility, hard countered by several characters and if she doesn't have her bubbles ready she is easy to kill
